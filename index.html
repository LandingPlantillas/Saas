❓ FAQ

Common questions.

How long does it take to set up? ▼
Most teams are fully set up within 20-30 minutes. We import your existing contacts, connect your email and walk you through the pipeline setup. No IT required.
Can I migrate from Salesforce or HubSpot? ▼
Yes. We have one-click migration tools for Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive and most other CRMs. Your data, contacts and deal history transfer in minutes.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes, 14 days free on all plans. No credit card required. Full access to all features including AI follow-ups and revenue forecasting.
How does the AI follow-up feature work? ▼
Flowly AI analyzes deal activity, timing and previous interactions to write and schedule personalized follow-up emails automatically. You review and approve before sending, or set it to fully automatic.
What integrations are available? ▼
Gmail, Outlook, Slack, LinkedIn, Zapier, Make, HubSpot, Stripe, Calendly and 40+ more. We add new integrations every month based on user requests.
